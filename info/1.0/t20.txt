 T20 World Cup - An app that helps you follow the ICC T20 Cricket World cup 2012 edition. Download & and follow the event with ease...! :wink:

Features:
1. Schedule to keep track of the event with ease.
Schedule will be updated as it progresses into knockout stages.
2. Points Table group-wise. As the tournament progresses into super-8 stage, the points table will reflect the super-8 standings.
3. Settings.
4. About. ( Here you can drop in your valueable suggestions, feedbacks... )


Upcoming features:
1. Live score updates of the ongoing matches
2. Push Notification for important milestones in the match.
3. Live score sharing feature using Facebook & Twitter.
